Study Partners – Chavrutot

The Jewish bookshelf reflects the world of Jewish ethics, thought and faith as it was developed over the generations by the Torah sages and great philosophers of the Jewish people. This bookshelf, like the entire world of the spirit it represents, belongs to every one of us. Each of us is invited to explore it, to open its books and become familiar with the content and philosophy on which the eternity of the Jewish people is based.

Everyone is capable of studying the Rambam, Rabbi Yehuda Halevi, the Maharal of Prague, Rav Kook, and all those great thinkers whose works fashioned the image of the Jewish people and continue to influence our way of life to this day.

The beit midrash of Yeshiva Ma’aleh Eliyahu is located in Tel Aviv, the business and cultural center of the State of Israel, and its door are open to any person interested in Jewish content: city residents, young and old, business people, those involved in culture and the arts – anyone who wants to meet, study and discuss. The study is conducted in the chavruta format – regular study encounters whose content, style and time frame are tailored to each person individually.

If you are seeking a close encounter with the world of content that makes the Jewish people unique;

If you want to become more familiar with Jewish philosophy and ethics;

If you are looking for a place where you can have a deep and authentic discussion about matters of the spirit,

You are invited to contact Tzvi at 052-6601990.
